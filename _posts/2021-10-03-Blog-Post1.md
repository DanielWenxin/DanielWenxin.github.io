```python
                  , height = 3.5, aspect = 1.3)

```


    
![output_3_0.png](/images/output_3_0.png)
    


The scatter plot reflects the Flipper Length (mm) and Body Mass (g) by different species. From the graph above, we can see that the body mass and flipper length of Gentoo penguins are significantly larger than those of the other two species. However, the body mass and flipper length distribution of Adelie and Chinstrap penguins are similar. Therefore it might not be optimal to distinguish the species of a given penguin through the combination of body mass and flipper length.
